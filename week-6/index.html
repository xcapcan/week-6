About Me

Hello, welcome to my website.

I chose this beach because it's my calm place. This is where I think a lot about life and my dreams. The beach is where my soul rests.

They say that the sea is endless. From a geographical point of view, this, of course, is not so. However, all doubts immediately disappear. The endless horizon is so vast, so far.

I like walks by the sea. They never bother, because they are always different. The sea itself is not the same. It is volatile in nature. It is his calm and quiet than his light waves. The eyes are warm, sunny and blind, not used to bright light, eyes.
